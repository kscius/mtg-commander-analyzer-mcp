# Voltron Strategy Guide

## Key principles

- Stack equipment and auras on the commander; commander damage is the primary win.
- Prioritize **hexproof**, **indestructible**, and **evasion** for the commander.
- Keep removal **instant-speed** to protect your setup.

## Recommended ratios

| Role | Count | Notes |
|------|-------|-------|
| Lands | 35–38 | Bracket 3 template |
| Ramp | 9–12 | Rocks and efficient mana |
| Equipment / auras | 12–18 | Core voltron package |
| Protection | 6–10 | Boots, greaves, shields |
| Evasion / equipment tutors | 4–6 | Connect for lethal |
| Removal | 5–7 | Instant-speed priority |
| Draw | 8–11 | Find pieces and gas |

## Core card types

- **Equipment**: Swiftfoot Boots, Lightning Greaves, premium swords
- **Auras**: only when commander survives combat (protection density)
- **Tutors**: equipment tutors within Bracket tutor caps

## Anti-patterns

- Too many symmetrical board wipes
- Slow five-mana engines with no equip support
- Destroy-all-equipment effects without redundancy

## Synergy packages

- **Equipment Suite**: greaves, boots, sigarda's aid, stoneforge mystic
- **Commander damage**: double strike, trample, power doublers

## Ideal curve

- Low MV rocks and protection; equipment at 2–4 MV
- Few creatures unless they tutor or protect equipment

## Commander impact

- Commanders with **built-in power**, **combat triggers**, or **equip discounts** need fewer redundant pieces.
- Multi-color commanders need fixing before expensive equipment.
