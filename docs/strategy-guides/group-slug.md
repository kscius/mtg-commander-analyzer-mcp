# Group Slug Strategy Guide

## Key principles

- **Everyone loses life** (or takes damage) repeatedly — drain the table.
- **Lifegain** on your side offsets symmetry; **payoffs** reward life loss/gain.
- **Politics**: you become the archenemy — include protection and wipes.

## Core card types

- **Slug**: Exsanguinate, Cut / Damnation variants, “each opponent loses”
- **Payoffs**: Bloodthirsty Conqueror, Ob Nixilis-style
- **Protection**: Ward, lifelink buffers

## Anti-patterns

- **Only symmetric** damage with no lifegain offset.
- **Too slow** — slug needs early chip damage.
- **Tokens/voltron** diluting slug density.
- Ignoring **artifact/enchantment** answers.

## Synergy packages

- **Extort + wide**: small creatures + Blind Obedience
- **Lifegain + drain**: Soul Warden + Exquisite Blood (check combo rules)
- **Commander combat + slug**: attack triggers that drain

## Ideal curve

- MV 2–4 core slug; finishers at 5–7 MV
- Keep enough interaction to survive while slugging

## Commander impact

- Orzhov/Rakdos commanders with **lifelink**, **attack drain**, or **spell drain** anchor the deck (e.g. Shadrix-style group slug themes).
- Match EDHREC slug `group-slug` for that commander.
