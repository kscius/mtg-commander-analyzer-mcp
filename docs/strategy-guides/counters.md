# +1/+1 Counters Strategy Guide

## Key principles

- **Place counters** on creatures; **proliferate** and **payoffs** multiply value.
- Prefer creatures that **scale** and **protect** countered bodies.
- Removal should be efficient — you invest in growing creatures.

## Core card types

- **Proliferate**: Karn’s Bastion, Inexorable Tide, Atraxa (if commander)
- **Payoffs**: Hardened Scales, Branching Evolution, Pir/Torbran-style
- **Placement**: Incubation Druid, Conclave Mentor

## Anti-patterns

- **-1/-1** or shrink effects in your own deck.
- Too many **non-creature** spells with no counter synergy.
- **Board wipes** without recovery or counter rebuild plan.
- Voltron-only plan ignoring counter synergies.

## Synergy packages

- **Doublers + proliferate**: Hardened Scales + proliferate source
- **Modular / inherit counters**: artifact creatures + sacrifice outlets (light aristocrat)
- **Infect** (only if commander/theme supports — check playgroup)

## Ideal curve

- MV 2–4 core: place counters and proliferate
- Finishers: large tramplers or “each counter” damage

## Commander impact

- Commanders that **distribute counters** or **proliferate** reduce need for redundant engines.
- Color pairs determine best payoffs (green + blue vs green + white).
